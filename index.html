The Baseline Audit

A 10-minute mirror that shows you the set-point your whole life keeps returning to.

Before you begin

There is a version of you that runs your life the moment nothing is asked of you. The one in the car after the engine turns off, at the kitchen bench when the house finally goes quiet. That version has a setting, and the setting decides more than you know.

What if the pattern was never a discipline problem.

This is not a test. There is no pass. This is a mirror. Answer for how you actually live, not how you intend to. The first answer is the true one.

The 90 second baseline check

Put one hand on your chest. Both feet on the floor. One slow breath. Now notice the state your body returns to when nothing is asked of it. Not the state you want. The one that is actually there.
